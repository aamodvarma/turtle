About Project Turtle


We are a dedicated team of university students who are passionately working towards the development of highly intricate machine learning models. Our primary goal is to contribute to the noble cause of turtle conservation in the Emirate of Abu Dhabi. By leveraging our expertise in machine learning, we aim to create cutting-edge solutions that can assist in locating turtle nests, thereby aiding the conservation efforts in the region.

Through extensive research and collaboration, we are developing intricate machine learning models that possess the capability to accurately identify and locate turtle nests. These models are designed to analyze various data sources, including satellite imagery, drone footage, and historical nesting patterns, to identify potential nesting sites. By leveraging pattern recognition and data analysis techniques, our models can identify subtle features and indicators that may indicate the presence of turtle nests.
